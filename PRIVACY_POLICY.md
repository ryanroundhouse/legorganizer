# Privacy Policy for Legorganizer

**Effective date:** March 3, 2026

Legorganizer is designed to run entirely on your device.

## Summary

- Legorganizer does **not** collect personal information.
- Legorganizer does **not** collect usage analytics.
- Legorganizer does **not** access contacts, location, camera, microphone, photos, files outside app storage, or other device data.
- Legorganizer does **not** send data to any server.
- Legorganizer does **not** share data with third parties.
- Legorganizer does **not** use advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or third-party analytics.

## Data storage

Any part/bin information you enter is stored locally on your device only (app-local storage). That data stays on your device unless you manually export it.

## Network activity

Legorganizer does not require an account and does not make outbound network requests for app operation.

## Apple App Store (App Privacy) declaration

For Apple's App Privacy labels, this app is intended to be submitted as:

- **Data Used to Track You:** None
- **Data Linked to You:** None
- **Data Not Linked to You:** None

## Google Play (Data Safety) declaration

For Google Play Data Safety, this app is intended to be submitted as:

- **No data collected**
- **No data shared**
- **Data is processed only on device (if any user-entered content exists, it remains local unless user exports manually)**

## Children's privacy

Legorganizer does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children, because it does not collect data from users.

## Changes to this policy

If app behavior changes in a future release (for example, if cloud sync or analytics are added), this policy will be updated before that release.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the contact method listed on the app's store page.
